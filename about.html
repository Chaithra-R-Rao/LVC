About Us

Live Virtual Class (LVC) is an innovative online learning platform tailored for professionals and aspiring learners. It offers expertly designed courses in cutting-edge technologies and methodologies, including:

MERN Stack: Master the modern web development suite comprising MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js to build robust and scalable applications.

MEAN Stack: Learn to leverage MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, and Node.js for creating dynamic, enterprise-grade web applications.

Generative AI: Dive into the world of artificial intelligence, focusing on cutting-edge generative models to design, implement, and innovate AI-driven solutions.

At LVC, learners can interact with experienced mentors, engage in hands-on projects, and access a wealth of resources to stay ahead in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.
