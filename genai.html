GEN AI

Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence systems capable of creating new content, such as text, images, music, or code, based on patterns learned from data.

It uses advanced machine learning models, like neural networks, to understand and generate outputs that resemble human creativity.

Applications of generative AI include chatbots, content creation tools, design assistance, and even drug discovery in healthcare.

Technologies like OpenAI’s GPT (for text) and DALL·E (for images) are prominent examples of generative AI systems.

While generative AI holds immense potential for innovation, it also raises ethical concerns, including copyright issues and the need for responsible use.
